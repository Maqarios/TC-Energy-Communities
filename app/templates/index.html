Who We Are

Hello, we are SolarEase, a dedicated team committed to transforming green energy into a viable and sustainable business model.

Identifying a problem

Green energy sources like solar produce little to no greenhouse gasses or other pollutants. This helps combat climate change and reduces air and water pollution, protecting ecosystems and biodiversity. It is also crucial for energy communities aiming to become self-sufficient and resilient. However, the transition to green energy is hindered by a lack of real-time data, revenue channels, and financial resources. Decisions are often based on rough estimates, and excess energy from PVs is sold to the main grid at minimal prices, making the model unsustainable.

Our solution

We propose autonomous mini-grids, reducing dependence on main utility providers. These mini-grids offer real-time data on energy production and consumption, financial tracking, and monthly projections. This enables efficient investment planning in PVs and strategic energy procurement. The mini-grid is equipped with sensors (such as temperature, sunlight and humidity), smart meters, and IoT devices to connect 6-8 buildings together. This mini-grid is being trained on historical data (in the form of energy bills from the last four years and the weather-related data available online). The system will be able to give data visualization about current status related to consumption and production of all buildings in a mini-grid while doing projections for the next one month (while taking the weather forecast values from the internet).

Power Mix

This website can calculate the power mix for a home given the start and end date of the period. The provided values are the overall consumption, the PV generation and how much energy has to be taken from the main grid, and how much can be sold to the main grid. This website calculates the investment needed and profit made for the given period and provides an in-depth cost analysis of the generation and main grid cost.
